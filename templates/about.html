✦ How It All Began

A Home Away From Home

JHD Hotel & Bar was founded with a simple belief: that a hotel should feel like coming home — but better. Set in New Bhopalpura, Udaipur, our property is built around comfort, cleanliness, and genuine hospitality.

Our 33 rooms span two categories — 22 Normal Rooms and 11 Super Deluxe Rooms — so every guest can find the right fit. We serve 100% pure vegetarian food, welcome pets, and pride ourselves on warm, attentive service.
